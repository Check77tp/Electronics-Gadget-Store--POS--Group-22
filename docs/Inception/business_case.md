# Business Case

## Problem Statement
Electronics retail businesses face challenges such as:
- Inaccurate inventory tracking
- Slow checkout process
- Poor reporting and sales analysis
- Lack of centralized sales records

## Proposed Solution
Develop a Point of Sale (POS) system that automates:
- Sales processing
- Inventory updates
- Report generation
- User management

## Expected Benefits
- Faster transaction processing
- Real-time inventory updates
- Accurate sales reporting
- Improved decision-making
- Reduced operational errors

## Business Value
The system will:
- Increase operational efficiency
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Reduce losses from stock mismanagement
- Support business growth through better analytics

## Cost Considerations
- Development time and effort
- Deployment infrastructure
- Maintenance and upgrades

The long-term operational benefits outweigh development costs.
