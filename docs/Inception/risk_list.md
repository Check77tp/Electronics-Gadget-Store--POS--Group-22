# Risk List and Feasibility Analysis

## Technical Risks
R1: Lack of experience with POS system architecture.  
R2: Integration challenges between modules (sales, inventory, reporting).  
R3: Data security vulnerabilities.  
R4: Incorrectly scanning of the product or gadget barcode.

## Business Risks
R4: System may not meet stakeholder expectations.  
R5: Budget or infrastructure limitations.



## Feasibility Analysis

### Technical Feasibility
The project is technically feasible using modern programming frameworks and databases.

### Economic Feasibility
Development costs are manageable within the academic project scope.

### Operational Feasibility
Store staff can be trained easily due to the system’s user-friendly design.
